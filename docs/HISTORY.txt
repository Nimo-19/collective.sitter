Pre-1.0 development
-------------------

This package was originally developed at the University's datacenter (HRZ) of
the Philipps-Universität Marburg, Hesse, Germany, starting in 2014. Original
developers were:

- Kristin Kuche
- Tobias Müllerleile
- Oliver Drewes

In 2020, the starzel.de team started working on the package and it was decided
to publish it within the Plone Collective on Github. This was done in
February, 2021.
